Detailed Analysis by Use Case

For Small Team Building a Business (Your Scenario)

ClickUp - Best Overall for Trillium ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Why it fits:

  • ✅ Amy's preference confirmed in meetings - strong automation with Greenhouse/Slack
  • ✅ Best HubSpot integration (native, bi-directional)
  • ✅ Excellent client-facing features for consultant/client collaboration
  • ✅ Satisfies Keenan's Kanban needs + Amy's advanced automation needs
  • ✅ Price-to-feature ratio strongest at $7/user for Unlimited plan
  • ✅ Can consolidate multiple tools into one platform
  • ⚠️ Fireflies integration requires Zapier, not native
  • ⚠️ Steeper learning curve due to feature depth
Recommendation: Start with Unlimited plan ($35/mo for 5 users) - gets you all essential features including HubSpot integration

Motion - Best for Productivity & AI ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Why it fits:

  • ✅ James's strong recommendation - he uses it daily
  • ✅ Best AI features: auto-scheduling, AI Employees, built-in meeting assistant
  • ✅ Solves Ryan's "meeting notes → tasks" problem natively (no Fireflies needed)
  • ✅ Best for individual productivity and time management
  • ⚠️ Weaker integrations (no native HubSpot/Greenhouse)
  • ⚠️ Limited client-facing capabilities
  • ⚠️ Higher individual cost ($29-49/mo per person)
Recommendation: Consider for Ryan/Amy individually if productivity is the top priority, or wait 6 months to see if integration ecosystem improves

Asana - Best for Structured Growth ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Why it fits:

  • ✅ Mature, enterprise-ready platform
  • ✅ Strong Goals/Portfolios for tracking business metrics
  • ✅ Excellent reporting and analytics
  • ✅ Good integrations across the board
  • ⚠️ More expensive ($10.99/user minimum for key features)
  • ⚠️ Less flexible than ClickUp or Notion
  • ⚠️ AI features less advanced than Motion
Recommendation: Consider if you prioritize stability & reporting over customization

Monday.com - Best for Visual/Non-Technical Teams ⭐⭐⭐

Why it fits:

  • ✅ Most intuitive interface - lowest learning curve
  • ✅ Strong automation (best auto-reporting per meeting notes)
  • ✅ Visual/colorful - great for visual thinkers
  • ⚠️ Can get expensive as you scale ($19/user for Pro features)
  • ⚠️ Less flexibility than ClickUp
  • ⚠️ Storage limits can be restrictive
Recommendation: Consider if team is non-technical and values visual simplicity over power features

Notion - Best for Documentation-Heavy Teams ⭐⭐⭐

Why it fits:

  • ✅ Ryan already familiar with it (from startup days)
  • ✅ Best for creating client-facing content/templates
  • ✅ Excellent knowledge management (docs, wikis, databases)
  • ✅ Most flexible for custom workflows
  • ⚠️ Weaker PM-specific features vs dedicated tools
  • ⚠️ AI costs extra ($10/user/mo on top)
  • ⚠️ Limited HubSpot/Greenhouse integrations
Recommendation: Use as documentation hub alongside ClickUp rather than primary PM tool

Final Recommendation for Trillium Team

Primary Choice: ClickUp (Unlimited Plan)

Total Cost: $35/month for 5 users (annual billing)

Reasoning:

  1. ✅ Amy's requirements met: Best automation, Greenhouse/Slack integration, client-facing features
  2. ✅ Keenan's requirements met: Kanban boards + much more as team grows
  3. ✅ HubSpot priority: Native integration ready when you launch HubSpot next week
  4. ✅ Best consolidation: Can replace multiple tools, reducing total SaaS spend
  5. ✅ Client work: Excellent external sharing for consultant-client collaboration
  6. ✅ Price: Best value at $7/user/month for feature set

Implementation Plan:

  • Week 1: Ryan sets up basic structure, Keenan gets Kanban boards
  • Week 2: Connect HubSpot integration
  • Week 3: Set up Fireflies→ClickUp via Zapier for meeting notes
  • Week 4: Create client-facing templates and train Amy on automation

Supplemental Option: Motion for Ryan individually

Cost: $29/month (individual, annual)

If Ryan's primary pain point is meeting notes → tasks and calendar management, consider Motion for him personally while team uses ClickUp. The AI scheduling and built-in meeting assistant would specifically help with his overwhelm managing multiple stakeholder meetings.

Total Cost: $35 (ClickUp for team) + $29 (Motion for Ryan) = $64/month
